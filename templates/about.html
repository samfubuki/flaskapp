Diabetes Prediction

This is a diabetes prediction application. Here, two factors are considered for predicting whether the person entering has diabetes or not. First is the count of the fasting sugar. If it exceeds a certain number, then the person might be having diabetes. Next, is the check whether the person is having frequent urination or not. If it is true, then the person might be having diabetes. If it is false, the person might not be having diabetes. These two factors have been taken based on the dataset and applying various prediction models to it.
